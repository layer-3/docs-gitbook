Only include methods that are currently enabled and available to users.

### Depositing $YELLOW into Yellow

To use $YELLOW inside the platform (staking, fee offsets, rewards tracking), you may need to deposit it.

Deposit flow:

1. Select token: $YELLOW
2. Select network (must match your wallet network)
3. Send $YELLOW to the deposit address / contract shown in the UI
4. Wait for confirmations
5. Balance appears in your Yellow account ledger

Common deposit failures:

* wrong network selected
* sending from an exchange without memo/tag support (if required)
* insufficient gas for token transfer
