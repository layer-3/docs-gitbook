# referral program

Refer users to Yellow. Earn a share of their trading fees.

## The deal

Partners earn **20–50%** of the trading fees paid by users they refer.

Paid in **USDT**, no matter what currency the original fee was collected in.

For **12 months** from each referred user's first trade.

Paid from the **referral bucket** of the [Fee Split](/broken/pages/5f83c56dc4cb42e1fa30db2a4b50914e641b8c37). You are not paid on top of the user's fee — you are paid from it.

## Terms

* single-level only — you earn from users you directly referred, not from their referrals
* no volume thresholds, no tier ladder — every partner gets the same base rate
* higher rates available case-by-case for top performers
* commission stops after 12 months from the referred user's first trade

## What your users get

When you create an invite code, you choose what your referred users get from signing up under it:

* a starting **VIP tier** — lower maker/taker fees from their first trade. See [VIP Tiers](/broken/pages/351b2a5bc03e7765456fedfbc674283a2a202b56).
* or a **flat fee discount** — a percentage off their trading fees

You're sharing part of your commission to do this. The Partner Dashboard shows the trade-off in real time.

## How to become a partner

The partner program is operated by Yellow's Business Development team. Reach out via Community channels or your Yellow contact.

## For users with a referral link

If you signed up through someone's link, the benefit is already attached to your wallet — either a starting VIP tier or a fee discount, depending on the link you used. You don't need to do anything.
