# Referral Program

| Tier     | 30d Volume   | Base Commission | <p>Reward</p><p>(L1)</p> | Max Kickback (L2) | Min Kickback (L2) |
| -------- | ------------ | --------------- | ------------------------ | ----------------- | ----------------- |
| Bronze   | < $100k      | 20%             | 15%                      | 5%                | 0%                |
| Silver   | $100k - $5M  | 25%             | 15-20%                   | 10%               | 0%                |
| Gold     | $5M - $50M   | 35%             | 20-35%                   | 15%               | 0%                |
| Platinum | $50M - $250M | 45%             | 25-45%                   | 20%               | 0%                |
| Diamond  | > $250M      | 50%             | 25-50%                   | 25%               | 0%                |

* Base Commission: total percentage of the trading fees generated by your referrals that you are eligible to receive from the platform.
* Kickback: the portion of user’s commission that he chose to share back with referral to incentivize them to trade. It acts as a "cashback" or discount for the trader.
