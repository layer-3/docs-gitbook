# Referral Program

Yellow's referral program lets partners earn a share of trading fees from users they bring to the platform, and lets partners pass a portion of that share back to their referred users as a fee discount.

## Commission Rate

Partners earn a **flat fixed % commission** on the trading fees generated by their referred users.

The commission is paid in USDT, no matter the fee was collected in.

## One Level, One Rate

You earn commission on trades made by users you directly referred.

Multi-level structures are planned for a later release and will use a separate points-based mechanism.

## Lifespan

Commission earnings on each referred user last **12 months**, rolling from that user's first trade.

After 12 months the referral relationship concludes. Yellow reserves the right to review program terms periodically; any changes are communicated in advance.

## Partner Configuration

Partners can configure the following from the **Partner Dashboard**:

* **user-facing fee discount** — share part of your fixed commission with your referred users as a fee discount, to incentivize them to trade
* **sub-partner commission** — if you onboard other partners under your account, set the commission you pass to them (within your own rate)
* **referral links** — generate basic or customized referral links and track attribution
* **payout tracking** — daily and cumulative earnings, per currency

## For Referred Users

If you signed up through a referral link or invite code:

* the partner who referred you may choose to share part of their commission with you as a fee discount on your trades
* the discount, if any, is set by the partner and visible in your fee summary
* the referral relationship is attached to your account at sign-up; you do not need to do anything to maintain it

## Points Layer

In addition to cash commission, partner activity generates **points**. Points convert to $YELLOW at a conversion rate set per season.

The points layer complements cash commission. It does not replace it in v1.

Season conversion rates are published separately.

## How It Fits With Fees

Referral commissions are paid from the **trading fees collected on referred users' trades**. They do not affect the fee schedule shown in [Fees](../trading-on-yellow/fees.md) — what changes is where a portion of that fee is routed after collection.

See [Fee Split](../trading-on-yellow/fee-split.md) for the full allocation model.

## What This Means in Practice

* partners earn a percentage of trading fees generated by their referred users for 12 months
* partners decide how much of that to share with their users as a fee discount
* there is no tier system in v1 — every partner gets the same base rate
* a points layer runs alongside cash commission and converts to $YELLOW seasonally
