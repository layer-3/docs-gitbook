# Quick Start

#### Step 1: Log In or Sign Up

To get started with Yellow, log in or create your account from the sign-in window.

You can connect using:

* **Google**
* **MetaMask**
* **Phantom**
* **Rabby**
* **WalletConnect**

Choose the option that works best for you and complete the login process.

{% hint style="warning" %}
**Important:** Always verify that you are signing in through the official Yellow interface.
{% endhint %}

#### Step 2: Deposit Funds to Your Account Balance

This process brings your assets onto the Yellow platform and makes them available in your **Account Balance**.

1. **Choose your asset and network.** On the deposit page, select the cryptocurrency you want to deposit and the corresponding blockchain network.
2. **Copy your Yellow deposit address.** A unique deposit address and QR code will be displayed for the selected asset and network.
3. **Initiate the transfer from your external wallet or exchange.** Paste the Yellow deposit address into the withdrawal field and carefully review all details before confirming.
4. **Wait for onchain confirmation.** The blockchain network will process and confirm the transaction. Confirmation time may vary depending on network conditions.
5. **Funds appear in your Account Balance.** Once confirmed, your assets will automatically appear in your **Account Balance**.

{% hint style="warning" %}
**Important:** Your **Account Balance** is your **Yellow Wallet** balance. Always make sure the selected asset and network match the sending wallet or exchange. Sending funds through the wrong network may result in permanent loss of assets.
{% endhint %}

#### Step 3: Transfer Funds to Your Trading Account

To make deposited funds available for trading, transfer them from your **Account Balance** to your **Trading Account**.

1. **Open the Transfer section** in the Yellow platform.
2. **Enter the transfer details.** Select the asset, specify the amount, and confirm the direction of transfer from **Account Balance** to **Trading Account**.
3. **Confirm the transfer.** Review the details and submit the transfer.
4. **Funds become available for trading.** Once completed, the assets will appear in your **Trading Account** and can be used in the market.

{% hint style="warning" %}
**Important:** The **Transfer** tab supports both directions: **Account Balance → Trading Account** and **Trading Account → Account Balance**.
{% endhint %}

#### Step 4: Place Your First Trade

Once your funds are in your **Trading Account**, you are ready to trade.

1. **Select a market** from the market list.
2. **Choose an order type** in the order form.
3. **Enter price and amount** for your trade.
4. **Click “Buy” or “Sell”** to place your order.

Your order will appear in **Open Orders** until it is filled or canceled.

#### Step 5: Withdraw Funds

To withdraw funds to an external wallet, you must use the reverse flow.

1. **Transfer funds back to your Account Balance.** Move the asset you want to withdraw from your **Trading Account** to your **Account Balance** using the **Transfer** tab.
2. **Open the Withdraw section.**
3. **Enter the destination address.** Withdrawals can be sent to any external address.
4. **Select the asset, network, and amount** you want to withdraw.
5. **Confirm the withdrawal** and wait for onchain processing.

Once processed, the funds will be sent to the destination address you entered.

{% hint style="info" %}
**Important:** You cannot withdraw directly from your **Trading Account**. To withdraw externally, you must first transfer funds to your **Account Balance**, then complete the withdrawal from the **Withdraw** tab.
{% endhint %}

{% hint style="warning" %}
**Security Note:** Withdrawal fees are paid from your **Account Balance**. If there is not enough balance available to cover the required network fee, the withdrawal may fail.
{% endhint %}
