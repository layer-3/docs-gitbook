# Connect Your Wallet

{% stepper %}
{% step %}
### Connect

Go to [yellow.pro](https://yellow.pro/) and click the **“Connect”** button in the top-right corner of the screen.
{% endstep %}

{% step %}
### Choose Your Login Method

A pop-up will appear with the available login and wallet connection options. Select the method you want to use.

Supported options include:

* **MetaMask**
* **Phantom**
* **Rabby**
* **WalletConnect**
* **Google**

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/chrome-capture-2026-3-14 (1).gif" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>
{% endstep %}

{% step %}
### Complete the Connection

If you choose a wallet-based option, your wallet will prompt you to approve the connection to Yellow. Click **“Approve”** or **“Connect”** in your wallet to continue.

If you choose **Google**, follow the sign-in flow and complete authentication in the pop-up window.

Once the connection is complete, your account will appear in the top-right corner of the screen, indicating that you are successfully logged in.
{% endstep %}
{% endstepper %}

{% hint style="warning" %}
Important: Yellow is non-custodial. Connecting your wallet does not give Yellow control over your funds — it only allows the platform to recognize your wallet address and request signatures when needed. If you sign in with Google, a wallet is created for you behind the scenes so you can still use Yellow in a non-custodial way.
{% endhint %}

### Disconnect or Log Out

To disconnect from Yellow:

1. go to the top-right corner of the screen
2. click on your balance
3. in the pop-up window, click **“Disconnect”**

You do not need to sign any transactions to disconnect from the platform.
