# Place a Trade

### Step 1: Select a Market

On the left side of the trading interface, you will see a list of available markets. Click on the market you want to trade, such as `ETH-USDT`.

### Step 2: Choose an Order Type

In the order form, select the type of order you want to place.

Available order types include:

* **Market Order** — executes immediately at the best available price
* **Limit Order** — executes at your selected price or better

For this example, use a **Limit Order**.

### Step 3: Enter the Price and Amount

In the order form:

* enter your desired price in the **Price** field
* enter the quantity you want to trade in the **Amount** field

Review the values carefully before submitting the order.

### Step 4: Click “Buy” or “Sell”

Once your order details are set, click **“Buy”** or **“Sell”** to submit the order.

After submission, your order will appear in the **Open Orders** section at the bottom of the screen.

If the order is filled, your balances will update accordingly and the trade will appear in your trade history.
