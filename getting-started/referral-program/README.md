# Referral Program

Two ways to use Yellow's referral program:

* invite a friend → they get a lower fee from day one, and you get credit for the referral
* become a partner → earn a share of trading fees from everyone you bring in, paid in USDT

## Invite a Friend

### How do I refer someone?

* Connect your wallet.
* Open the Referral page.
* Copy your link or tap a share button (Telegram, X).

Your link looks like:

`app.yellow.pro?ref=YOUR_CODE`

Anyone who signs up through that link is attached to your account.

### What does my friend get?

A lower starting fee on Yellow — a [VIP Tier](/broken/pages/947c48da8cb67066c8ac4835b989aab4db4931dc) above Base from their first trade. The benefit applies automatically once they connect their wallet — they don't need to enter anything manually.

## Become a Partner

The partner program is for people who actively drive sign-ups — KOLs, traders with a following, community leaders, content creators.

### What you earn

* A fixed percentage of the trading fees paid by users you refer.
* Paid in USDT, no matter what currency the original fee was in.
* For 12 months from each referred user's first trade.

That's it. No tiers, no volume thresholds. Higher rates can be granted case-by-case to top performers.

### How it works

* Get onboarded by Yellow's Business Development team (see "How to apply" below).
* Get access to the Partner Dashboard.
* Generate invite codes — one per campaign, one per sub-partner, however you want to organize.
* Share your codes. Earnings start the moment your referred users trade.

### What you can give your referred users

When you create an invite code, you choose what your users get from signing up under it:

* a starting [VIP tier](/broken/pages/947c48da8cb67066c8ac4835b989aab4db4931dc) — lower maker/taker fees from their first trade, no volume needed
* or a flat fee discount — a percentage off their trading fees

You're sharing part of your commission to do this. The Partner Dashboard shows the trade-off in real time so you can balance acquisition against margin.

### A worked example

Say you have a referred user trading $100,000 in volume in a month, paying $100 in trading fees. Your partner rate is 20%. You earn $20 in USDT for that month. If you set their code to a 5% fee discount, they paid $95 instead of $100, you still earn 20% of the $95 → $19.

(Numbers illustrative. Your actual rate is set during onboarding.)

### Partner Dashboard

Everything you need is in one place:

* Overview — total partners, traders, volume, and earnings over the last 7d / 30d / 90d / all time
* Partners — the KOLs or sub-partners you've onboarded, their performance, and the codes they're using
* Invited Traders — every end-user across all your partners, with their volume and the fees they've generated
* Codes — every invite code you've issued, who it's linked to, and how it's performing
* Payout History — when you got paid, how much, and your next scheduled payout

### Sub-partners

You can invite other partners to work under your account (KOLs, regional teams, sub-affiliates):

* Share your onboarding link from the Invite Partner card.
* When they open the link, their wallet appears in your pending list.
* Issue them an invite code to activate them.
* You set their commission rate. They earn from the users they bring in; you earn an override on their activity.

Single-level commissions apply (L1 only): you earn from users your sub-partners refer, not from users referred by their sub-partners. Multi-level reach is planned for a later release through a points-based mechanism.

### Points

Partner activity also earns points, separate from your cash commission. Points convert to $YELLOW at a rate set per season.

Cash commission and points run in parallel. Both grow with the volume you generate.

### How to apply

The partner program is operated by Yellow's Business Development team. Reach out via Community channels or your existing Yellow contact.

## If You Signed Up Through Someone's Link

Welcome. You don't need to do anything — the referral is already attached to your wallet.

Depending on the link you used, you may have:

* a starting [VIP tier](/broken/pages/947c48da8cb67066c8ac4835b989aab4db4931dc), with lower maker/taker fees from your first trade
* or a fee discount that applies to every trade

Your applied benefit shows up in your fee summary. The base fee schedule still lives on the Fees page, and the full tier table is on [VIP Tiers](/broken/pages/947c48da8cb67066c8ac4835b989aab4db4931dc).

## How This Relates to Yellow's Fee Flow

Trading fees you pay aren't changed by referrals. What changes is what happens to those fees after they're collected — a portion is routed to the partner who referred you, and the rest follows Yellow's normal allocation. The full breakdown is on the Fee Split page.
