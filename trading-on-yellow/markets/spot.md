# Spot

Spot trading is the direct exchange of one asset for another, for example buying BTC with USDT.

When a spot order is filled:

* your balances update on the platform after execution
* the trade is completed within Yellow’s trading environment, so you can continue trading using your updated balance

Onchain asset movement happens when you deposit or withdraw:

* when you deposit, assets become available for trading on Yellow
* when you withdraw, assets are sent back to your wallet
* assets are not moved onchain for each individual trade

### What This Means in Practice

* when a spot order is filled, your available balances update for further trading
* onchain transactions are tied to deposits and withdrawals, not every trade
