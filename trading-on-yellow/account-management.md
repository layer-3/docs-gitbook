# Account Management

Your Yellow account includes:

* balances
* open orders
* fills (trade history)
* transfer history (deposits and withdrawals)

### Balances

You may see multiple balance values:

* **Total** — all funds credited to your account
* **Available** — funds that can be used now for new orders or withdrawals
* **Reserved** — funds currently locked for open orders

Examples:

* spot buy orders reserve quote currency
* spot sell orders reserve base currency

### Orders

You should be able to:

* view open orders
* cancel orders
* filter by market and status
* review order history, including filled, canceled, and rejected orders

### History

Separate history views are useful for:

* fills (trades)
* transfers (deposits and withdrawals)
* fees

Yellow should show timestamps, amounts, asset, and status for each entry.
