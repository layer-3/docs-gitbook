# Troubleshooting

### Deposits

<details>

<summary>My deposit is confirmed, but I do not see it</summary>

First, **log out and sign in again**.

In some cases, a processed deposit will not appear in the interface until you re-login.

</details>

<details>

<summary>My deposit still does not appear after re-login</summary>

If the deposit is confirmed onchain and still does not appear after re-login, contact [.](./ "mention") and share Trading Account address, transaction hash, asset, amount and network.

</details>

### Transfers

<details>

<summary>My funds are in Account Balance, but I cannot trade</summary>

If your funds are in **Account Balance**, you must transfer them to **Trading Account** before trading.

Use the **Transfer** tab and select:

**Account Balance → Trading Account**

</details>

<details>

<summary>My transfer back is still pending</summary>

If you transferred funds from **Trading Account** to **Account Balance** and the transfer is still pending, wait for the status to update.

If it remains pending longer than expected, contact [.](./ "mention") and include asset, amount, your **Trading Account address,** transfer hash.

{% hint style="info" %}
**Important:** Moving funds from **Trading Account** to **Account Balance** does not complete the withdrawal by itself. After that, you still need to use the **Withdraw** tab.
{% endhint %}

</details>

### Withdrawals

<details>

<summary>My withdrawal is pending</summary>

Withdrawals can complete in **a few seconds**, but in some cases they can take **up to 1 day**. A withdrawal may stay pending because of processing conditions, retry timing, or platform limits.

If the status does not change for longer than expected, contact support and include the withdrawal details, as well as trading account address and transaction hash.

</details>

<details>

<summary>My withdrawal is delayed</summary>

A withdrawal may be delayed because of:

* hourly limits
* daily limits
* retry timing
* additional security verification

If an hourly limit is reached, the withdrawal may retry in about **1 hour**. If a daily limit is reached, it may be retried the **next day**.

</details>

<details>

<summary>My withdrawal failed after multiple attempts</summary>

Repeated attempts can trigger additional security verification.

If this happens, stop retrying and contact [.](./ "mention").

</details>

### Trading

<details>

<summary>My funds are stuck after a trade</summary>

If your available balance does not update correctly after a trade, part of your funds may remain temporarily unavailable. Refresh the page and check your trade history first. If the issue continues, contact [.](./ "mention") with your Trading Account address, the asset, the amount, and a screenshot if possible.

</details>
