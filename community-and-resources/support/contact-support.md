# Contact Support

If you can't find the answer you're looking for in our [faq.md](faq.md "mention")and [troubleshooting.md](troubleshooting.md "mention"), you can contact our support team directly using preffered way:

* Send an email to support@yellow.pro.
* Join our official Discord server and open a support ticket: [https://discord.com/invite/yellownetwork](https://discord.com/invite/yellownetwork) > Yellow.pro > "Support" server.

Our support team is committed to providing timely assistance. We strive to respond to all inquiries within 24 hours.
